Walt Disney World is so cool stuff

geolocation

Plan Details

Get to Hollywood Studios first and immediately run to Toy Story Mania when the park opens. Grab a FastPass, then jump in the regular line (no FastPass). As soon as you get out, use your FastPass to go back through again! After TSM, walk on over to Rockin’ Rollercoaster and get in line to scream with Steven Tyler & Aerosmith. Don’t bother riding this if you get motion sickness at all... Once you’re out of RR, head toward Tower of Terror nearby and spend some quality time with Rod and the gang 199 ft up. Again, do avoid this if you’re prone to motion sickness! At this point, if you’ve moved quickly and not wasted time, it should be midmorning and about time to have a snack. I strongly recommend Starring Rolls (near RR) for a quick bite!
